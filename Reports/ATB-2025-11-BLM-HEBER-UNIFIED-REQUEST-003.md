# ATB TECHNICAL MEMORANDUM
## Unified Federal Land Control Request – Heber Autonomous Campus

Document ID: ATB-2025-11-BLM-HEBER-UNIFIED-REQUEST-003

---

## 1. Purpose

Request coordinated federal land control (lease / special-use / ROW) over the
federally owned lands in T13N R16E, Navajo County, Arizona, comprising:

- Parcel 201-02-004 – 15,360 acres – BLM – Sections 1–24.
- Parcel 201-02-005 – 6,282.11 acres – BLM – Sections 25–36 (east).
- Parcel 201-02-006 – 1,280 acres – USA – southwest infill of Sections 25–36.

Total combined planning area: approximately 23,000 acres.

## 2. Requested Action

Spear Enterprise LLC requests long-term authorization to develop and operate a
renewable energy, hydrogen, and aerospace campus across these lands, including:

- Solar generation up to 1.5–2.0 GW.
- Hydrogen production, storage, and fuel cell systems.
- Vertical launch tower(s) and associated aerospace facilities.
- Supporting industrial, research, and limited habitation infrastructure.

## 3. Public Interest and Safety Justification

- All high-energy activities occur fully within federal land with multi-kilometer
  standoff from private property and from Camp Ponderosa.
- The project advances national objectives in clean energy, hydrogen storage,
  and space access while concentrating impacts on a single, well-managed site.
- Southern parcels function primarily as safety exclusion zones and wildfire
  buffers.

## 4. Next Federal Steps

1. Confirm agency jurisdiction for parcel 201-02-006 and designate a lead
   coordination office.
2. Initiate pre-application discussions with BLM Safford Field Office and
   Sitgreaves National Forest representatives.
3. Define NEPA EA scope for the unified campus.
