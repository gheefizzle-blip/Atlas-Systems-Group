# 1. Mission Overview

The OSY (Orbital Shipyard) Lunar Production Plan defines the full manufacturing and integration architecture required to support lunar logistics, construction, and base deployment.  
OSY serves as the **primary fabrication center** for:

- LTS-1 / LTS-2 / LTS-3 shuttle fleets  
- Lunar Cargo Modules (LCM-S/M/L)  
- Robotic Lunar Construction Division (RLCD) units  
- Cislunar Depot modules  
- Habitat elements and utility modules  
- Solar and power arrays  
- Structural and truss components  

OSY is the industrial heart of the lunar program.

# 3. Production Pipeline Architecture

The OSY lunar production line is divided into **six major pipelines**:

---

## Pipeline A — Vehicle Frame & Structure

### Output:
- LTS-1, LTS-2, LTS-3 core frames  
- Depot structural truss segments  
- Habitat pressure hulls  

### Processes:
- Zero-G truss welding  
- Automated beam fabrication  
- Carbon composite shell layup  
- Micrometeoroid shielding attachment  

---

## Pipeline B — Propulsion & Tank Module Fabrication

### Output:
- Engine assemblies for all LTS classes  
- NH3 tanks  
- LOX cryo-tanks  
- Depot propellant storage  

### Processes:
- Composite tank winding  
- Cryo-insulation bonding  
- Valve module installation  
- Engine module pressure testing  

---

## Pipeline C — Avionics & Autonomy Integration

### Output:
- Flight computers  
- Navigation cameras  
- LIDAR/terrain mapping units  
- TRN & star-tracker packages  
- Depot control electronics  

### Processes:
- Harness routing  
- PCB mounting  
- Redundant bus testing  
- EMI/thermal chamber testing  

---

## Pipeline D — LCM Production (Container Lines)

### Output:
- LCM-S, LCM-M, LCM-L standard containers  

### Processes:
- Frame assembly  
- Composite panel bonding  
- Robotic rail installation  
- QR/RFID marking  
- Environmental insulation  

---

## Pipeline E — Robotic Workforce Assembly

### Output:

### Processes:
- Chassis welding

## Pipeline F — Depot Module Fabrication

### Output:

# 4. Integrated Production Flow

### Step 1 — Raw Material Inbound
Metals, composites, electronics delivered by orbital tugs.

### Step 2 — Material Staging
Automated sorting into dedicated production pipelines.

### Step 3 — Subassembly Fabrication
Each pipeline produces modular components.

### Step 4 — Final Assembly Cells
OSY uses robotic clamps and automated cranes to mate:
- Frames  
- Tanks  
- Avionics  
- Power systems  
- Landing legs  
- Cargo bays  

### Step 5 — Test & Certification
- Pressure tests  
- Thrust tests  
- Autonomy simulations  
- Vacuum exposure  
- Thermal cycle validation  

### Step 6 — Deployment
Completed LTS, LCMs, robots, or depot modules are:
- Loaded onto orbital tugs  
- Sent to cislunar depots  
- Entered into MECSAI operations
